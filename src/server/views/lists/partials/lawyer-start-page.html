The Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO) has created a list of lawyers abroad to help British nationals find the legal support they need. You can search lawyers by:

  • country
  • region
  • area of expertise

All lawyers on the list have been contacted by the FCDO to make sure they have:

  • good English language skills
  • legal qualifications
  • up to date contact details

It is recommended that you own research before deciding which lawyer to use.

Lawyers feedback

The FCDO welcomes feedback from people who have used lawyers from the list. We use this feedback to make sure all information is up to date and accurate.

The FCDO will quickly respond to feedback and will remove any lawyer from the list where appropriate.

Find a medical facility abroad

The Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office (FCDO) has created a list of medical facilities abroad to help British nationals find the medical care they need. You can search medical by:

  • country
  • region

It is recommended that your own research before deciding which medical facility to use.

Medical facility feedback

The FCDO welcomes feedback from people who have used a medical facility from the list. We use this feedback to make sure all information is up to date and accurate.

The FCDO will quickly respond to feedback and will remove any medical facility from the list where appropriate.

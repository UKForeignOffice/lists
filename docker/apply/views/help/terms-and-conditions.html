Find a lawyer abroad

To be considered for the ‘Find a lawyer abroad’ service you must:

  • be currently qualified to practise law or meet the national criteria for providing legal services to the public, for example mandatory affiliation
  • have an acceptable level of written and spoken English
  • not be disbarred from practising law
  • be reliable and willing to help on a regular basis
  • be able to respond within a reasonable timeframe to client requests
  • be willing to update or re-confirm your details on an annual basis on request

Find a funeral director abroad

To be considered for the ‘Find a funeral director abroad’ service you must be:

  • currently qualified to provide funeral services, or are currently registered with the local authorities to provide funeral services (if applicable in your country)
  • reliable and willing to help on a regular basis
  • able to respond within a reasonable timeframe to client requests
  • willing to update or re-confirm your details on an annual basis on request

Find a translator or interpreter abroad

To be considered for the ‘Find a translator or interpreter abroad’ service you must be:

  • currently qualified to provide translation or interpretation services
  • reliable and willing to help on a regular basis
  • able to respond within a reasonable timeframe to client requests
  • willing to update or re-confirm your details on an annual basis on request

FCDO will consider removing you from the list should it find that you

  • are subject to any adverse finding or action in respect of your professional services
  • are not currently qualified to operate the service
  • are unreliable, or fail to respond within a reasonable timeframe to client requests
  • are unwilling to help on a regular basis
  • charge inconsistent fees
  • are found to be displaying any FCDO logo or branding on your website, or seek to imply that you or your company have been endorsed by the FCDO
